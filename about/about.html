Investing in Educational Excellence for Students in District 109

Get Involved

The Deerfield Education Foundation is a 501(c)(3) not-for-profit, volunteer organization made up of educators and community members who sponsor programs and projects that improve the educational experience for students within District 109. If you are interested in getting involved, come to our next Board meeting at the District 109 administrative office on Thursday, May 26, 2016 at 8:45 am. Get Involved

Amazon Smile

AmazonSmile is a charitable program offered through Amazon.com. By signing up, Amazon will donate 0.5% of the price of your eligible AmazonSmile purchases to the Deerfield Education Foundation. Visit smile.amazon.com for details. The Foundation is listed on AmazonSmile as Deerfield School District 109 Education Advancement Foundation. Please sign up today!

Why Donate?

Donations to the Deerfield Education Foundation are tax deductable and are used to improve the educational experience of students within District 109. In the past we have sponsored art and music events, purchased technology for the classroom, and contributed to facility improvements to improve the health and well being of students. We appreciate and rely on donations from people such as you and the Foundation would like to thank all of our past and future donors. Please click on the links below to make a donation today.
